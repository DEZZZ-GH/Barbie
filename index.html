Barbie Expands Diversity with New Dolls Celebrating Global Cultures


Mattel has unveiled its latest Barbie line, "Global Glamour," featuring dolls inspired by traditional attire from around the world, including India, Nigeria, and Mexico. The collection aims to promote cultural representation and inclusivity, with each doll designed in collaboration with local artisans. This launch follows Barbie's recent focus on diverse role models, such as career-themed dolls and historical figures, reinforcing the brand's commitment to empowering children through play.

Barbie Launches Eco-Friendly Doll Line Made from Recycled Materials


Mattel has introduced a new sustainable Barbie collection, "Barbie Loves the Ocean," featuring dolls made from 90% recycled ocean-bound plastic. The line includes three dolls with beach-themed outfits and accessories, all crafted with eco-conscious materials. This initiative is part of Barbie’s broader push toward sustainability, aligning with parent company Mattel’s goal to achieve 100% recycled or bio-based plastics in all products by 2030. The move highlights the brand’s effort to inspire environmental awareness in younger generations.
